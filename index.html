About

Passionately created by the students of the Summer 2020 session of Music in the United States, taught by Professor David Asbury at Southwestern University (Georgetown, Texas), this website was born out of a desire to present the curated playlist of protest music made for their final project. The goal of this project was to outline a brief history of social protest through song. Music has been a crucial component of resistance throughout history and has been a vital outlet for many of the students as they have navigated the challenges of todayâ€™s world. Included on this website are the songs and accompanying analysis that the students found most moving, as well as some personal reflections. Their hope for anyone visiting this website is that they enjoy the playlist, feel the power of music as resistance, and maybe even learn something.
